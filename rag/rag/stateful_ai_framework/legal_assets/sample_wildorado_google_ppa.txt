Section 7.3 Curtailment Compensation
If the project is curtailed by a grid operator instruction and the project was otherwise available to generate energy, the buyer shall compensate seller for eligible curtailed energy at the contract rate, subject to exclusions in Section 7.4.

Section 7.4 Exclusions
Curtailment compensation shall not apply during seller-caused outages, planned maintenance windows, force majeure events, telemetry failures, or periods where the project was not commercially available.

Section 8.2 Settlement Adjustment
If settlement records understate eligible compensated energy, seller may submit a billing dispute with supporting operational records, market instructions, and contract evidence within the allowed dispute window.

Schedule A Contract Rate
Contract rate: 42.50 USD per MWh for eligible compensated energy.
